Trenching and Underground services
Trenching and underground services are our specialty.

We offer overhead to underground conversions, power to new dwellings, trenching for combined services (eg water, gas, phone, fibre etc,) and cable repair. We have a trencher which can dig trenches up to several Kilometres if required.
Commercial and Industrial
We are available for office or shop fit outs, lighting upgrades, switchboard upgrades, power cabling, data cabling, testing and tagging, fault finding, security/emergency/exit lighting, repairs and more. With our own Electrical Inspector, we can complete the whole process.

Specialised services are available for new industrial project installations or maintenance.
Residential
We can assist you with new homes, renovations, alterations, maintenance or projects such as indoor or outdoor lighting upgrades, underfloor heating, switchboard replacements or any other household requirements. We can work with your plans or are happy to offer advice based on our years of experience.
